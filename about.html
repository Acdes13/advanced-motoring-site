About Advanced Motoring

Experienced, RSA-Approved Instruction

Hi, I’m Richard — the founder and instructor at Advanced Motoring School. With many years of experience helping learners succeed, I’ve built a school that’s centered around honesty, support, and success. Based in Navan, I specialise in manual lessons on the Navan test routes with a strong focus on EDT training, mock tests, and real-world driving confidence. I’m proud to have helped so many students pass first time and earn their independence.
